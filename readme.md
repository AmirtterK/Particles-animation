# Particles Animation

An interactive 3D particle system built with Three.js. Enter up to 12 characters and watch 80,000 particles fly in from deep space to spell out your text.

Live demo: https://amirtterk.github.io/Particles-animation/

## Features

- Type any word or name and the particles form it in 3D space
- Click or tap to explode the particles and watch them reform
- Move your cursor through the cloud to push particles around
- Click and drag to rotate the scene
- Ambient effects: outer ring, background stars, and shooting stars
